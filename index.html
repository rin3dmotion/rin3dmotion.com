Statement of Work

I develop detailed SOW to your product and brand style. This helps to ensure that every aspect of your project is accurately reflected in the work.

3D Render

I create clear and detailed 3D renderings that will allow you to present your ideas clearly. My task is to make your product visible and attractive to your target audience.

3D Animation

I make your product dynamic by revealing its features in motion. This improves the audience's perception of your brand, increasing engagement.

Post-Processing

During post-processing, I perform contrast correction and add effects. This stage ensures that the final image is fully consistent with the brand concept.
